# Chaos TV — v0.1.0-alpha

> ⚠️ **This is alpha software.** Expect rough edges, incomplete features, and occasional bugs. Use at your own discretion.

## About

Chaos TV is a Kotlin Multiplatform app for browsing and watching talks from [media.ccc.de](https://media.ccc.de) and live streams from [streaming.media.ccc.de](https://streaming.media.ccc.de). Available for Android phones, Android TV, and iOS.

## Highlights

- **Browse & Search** — Explore conferences and search events from the media.ccc.de archive
- **Live Streams** — Watch live streams from streaming.media.ccc.de
- **Playback History** — Resume talks where you left off with continue-watching support
- **Favorites** — Mark talks to find them easily later
- **Queue** — Build a playlist of talks to watch next, with drag-to-reorder support
- **Android TV** — Full D-pad navigation, dedicated TV player, and leanback experience
- **Picture-in-Picture** — Continue watching while using other apps (Android)
- **Localized** — Available in English, German, and Spanish
